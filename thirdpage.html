Covid-19 Background

Unfortunately, it is unknown when the case started. However, around 2019 of December, it was identified in China. As of January 2020, the World Health Organisation declared the outbreak as an international concern. The newly discovered virus is known as a severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2, also known as covid-19.


Covid-19 Origins

It was speculated that the virus first originated on 1st of October 2019, even though the earliest known case was on 1st of December 2019. Covid-19 is known to come from the wet market of Wuhan China. Scientist think that the coronavirus came from bats.


How Covid-19 Infects

According to the article written by the World Health Organisation, transmission happens when an individual comes in direct contact with someone who is infected via respiratory droplets, normally through coughing and sneezing. A person can also be infected if they have touched contaminated surfaces that contain the virus and happen to accidentally put their hands on their eyes,mouth etc. Unfortunately, the Covid-19 Virus can survive for several hours on surfaces but can easily get obliterated by disinfectants.


Covid-19 Treatments and Vaccinations

For serious cases where patients are in critical condition, doctors in the United Kingdom have administered a type of steroid, which according to them, provided benefits which increased their survivability, however does not prove to help in the patient's recovery. Also, studies regarding administering steroids were conflicting. Fortunately, there are a few vaccines available such as the Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine.


Covid-19 Preventions

According to the article written by the World Health Organisation, to prevent the spread of Covid-19, it is best to:
Wash hands with soap often
Maintain Safe Distancing
Wear A Mask
Prevent touching of Eyes,Nose,Nouth,
Cover nose and mouth with tissue when coughing or sneezing
Stay home if unwell
Seek medical attention if fever,coughing or difficulty breathing
