Covid-19 Singapore Status

According to the latest information from the website disease.sh, it is reported that there are around 81 MILLION cases of people
infected with covid-19 so far as of 2nd of february 2021. With the total deaths amounting to around 1.7 million,
it is crucial that we are not complacent regarding the current situation of the epidemic and to stay indoors as much as possible to avoid being exposed to the virus.
This website aims to not just inform but educate the masses regarding Covid-19, the possible origins, how it spreads, how it is being treated, how to prevent and so on!
